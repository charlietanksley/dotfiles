*scrollcolors.txt*            ScrollColors

A plugin to help you flip effortlessly through the installed color schemes.

                                              *SCROLL* *SCROLLCOLORS*
Type :SCROLL, then use the arrow keys to rotate through the colorschemes.
? for help, ESC to exit.

To quickly change colors, you can map keys to NextColor and PrevColor.

      map <silent><F2> :PREVCOLOR<cr>
      map <silent><F3> :NEXTCOLOR<cr>
